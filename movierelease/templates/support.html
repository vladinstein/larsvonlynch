Using the app.

1) Start typing (or copy and paste) the name of the movie*.
2) Select the movie from the list**.
3) Press "Add movie".
4) To remove the movie from your list press "Delete".
5) Go to the settings to adjust your notifications.

* try to use the exact name of the movie, with the correct punctuation marks.
** you can add only the movies that has not been released yet.

Notification time.

Right now there is no support of the timezones. So all the users are going to recieve their notification e-mails at the same time: UTC 8:00 AM. That equals to 11:00 AM Moscow Standard Time, 3:00 AM EST, 4:00 AM EDT, midnight PST and 1:00 AM PDT.
